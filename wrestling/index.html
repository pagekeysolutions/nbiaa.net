Registration

Northern Burlington Junior Greyhounds Athletic Association sponsors wrestling for youth ages 5-15 in the Northern Burlington County (NJ) Regional School District and surrounding communities.

This program serves as a feeder program for the Northern Burlington County Regional Middle and High Schools.

Registration forms are distributed through area schools each year.
